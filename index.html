Introduction


Welcome to my software development portfolio!

Below is a collection of projects which I believe demonstrate my experience in game engines, programming languages, and digital workspaces in general. I have provided brief summaries of my work, as well as links to their public pages.

Solar Sails

This was a team project where we were expected to brainstorm and take a game from concept to a playable demo. I was responsible for enemy design, controller setup, and a custom zero gravity movement system. The turrets and modular laser grids were my creations, as well as a prototype wall-jumping turret, and walking enemy, which unfortunately didnâ€™t make it into the final build.

Fireball Brawl

Originally a testing ground for my 3D unity ideas, I brought it forward as a basis for another team project. Together with my fellow students, we came up with a fighting game layout, and I was given the task of putting together a player, third person camera system, enemies, and an arena survival setup. The three elemental spells, Fire, Earth, and Water, were my creations as well.

Battle Snails

For this Game Jam project I was the lead programmer. The whole team collaborated on designing the game itself, however I was tasked with building it, including teaching a team member to work in Unity/Visual Studio to help with the menu UI, and implementing art assets from the rest of the team. My goal for the project was to take a dive into multiplayer setups in Unity, and I spent some time learning about the input system and setting up multiple players with different control devices. I also constructed the final arena using modular tiles, and set up the background and intro scroll animations from static art and sprites.
